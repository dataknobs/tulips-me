When is best time to see tulips in Ottawa? Describe how to make use of trip in spring season completed ? The best time to see tulips in Ottawa is during the tulip festival, which takes place in May. To make the most of your trip, plan to arrive in the city a few days before the festival begins. This will give you time to explore the city and visit some of the other attractions, such as the Canadian Museum of Nature and the National Gallery of Canada.

Tulip Festival in Washington is from April 1-April 30.

The Tulip Festival event is organized in North of Washington state.  The place is called Skagit Valley.  The festival is also known as Skagit Valley Tulip Festival.  With start of spring tulip flowers bloom.  Thousands of people visit everyday and enjoy the colorful flowers.

The farmers of Skagit Valley Washington grow Tulip in hundreds of acres. When flower bloom - the whole area is full of color - Red, Pink, White, Yellow tulips.

During the time of Tulip festival - various other activities are organized such as kite flying, fair, sale of painting, arts.

even if Tulip festival is for 1 month - it is so popular that it is described as one of top things to see in Washington.

If you are in Washington during April - plan for half a day to 1 day trip to tulip Festival.  Do not go on a rainy day.  Plan to go there on a sunny day especially between 10 AM-2PM.

When is best time to see tulips in Victoria BC? Describe Tulips Festival in detail Tulips Festival is a celebration of spring that takes place in Victoria, BC, Canada every year. The festival features over a million tulips in bloom, as well as other spring flowers. It is held for two weeks in April or May.

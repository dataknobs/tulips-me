Things to do and see at Skagit Valley Tulips Festival
Most important item at Skagit Valley Tulip festival is -
Buy a ticket and enter into Tulips field. Here you can walk, take pictures with Tulips.
Tulips garden in Washington state has 90+ varieties of tulips. The garden has 140 flower bulb varieties in total.
The varities include are tulips, daffodils, hyacinths, crocus, muscari, irises, and other specialty flowers.
There is art exhibition.
You can walk around Tulips field or take a tractor tour
Tractor will take you thru muddy field and you will enjoy complete view of Tulips
Near the Tulips field, you can fly kites
There is kite shop also in Tulips festival.

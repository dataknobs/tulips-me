Ballard Locks in Seattle gets more boat traffic than any other lock in the US.
Ballard Locks + fish ladder + surrounding Carl S. English, Jr. + Botanical Gardens attract more than one million visitors every year.
Because of Ballard Locks Seattle topograpgy is impacted. It lower the water level in lake washington and lake union by 8+ feet, create more water front land.
Ballard locks connect Lake Union and Lake Washington with the Puget Sound area.

GPS Address
3015 NW 54th St, Seattle, WA 98107
Tel: (206) 783-7059

When to visit
Best time to visit Ballard lock is a sunny day. You can see water level, boats and fish.
From Mid August to Mid September you can see large salmon fishes passing thru.
Ballard Locks are located in Salmon Bay, Seattle, Washington
Ballard Locks, are complex of locks at the west end of Salmon Bay. It is located in in Seattle, Washington's Lake Washington Ship Canal, between the neighborhoods of Ballard to the north and Magnolia to the south.

About Locks
Small boats, kayak can go thru Ballard locks. Large Boats of lentgth upto 760*80 feet can pass thru Locks.
Ballad Locks were built in 1911-1917.
When Boat reached in Balalrd locks, the water level is lowered and boat come to Puge Sound water level.
The water level can be lowered up to 26th feet.

Name

Ballard locks are also called Hiram M. Chittenden Locks
If you are travelling by boat and passing thru Ballard locks - it takes around 15 minute to switch from 1 water level to toehr and pass thru.
However depending on traffic there might be lot of boats waiting,so plan for waiting time.

Ticket Price

Hours

Visitors
As a visitor you not only can see Ballard locks, you can mechanics of water level and how boats are lowered from 1 water level to other.
Fish ladder : There is fish ladder of 21 steps that enable fishes to climb up to fresh water side. As a visitor you can see them closely thru glass window.
Next to Ballard lock is a park and botanical garden. It is called as - Carl S. English Jr. Botanical Garden . You can sit in park and see boats passing thru. It is good area for kids.
There is also a visitor center and a gift shop

Fish Market
Pike place has 4 fish markets. Here you can get fresh and smoked salmon, Alaskan halibut, Tuna, Shrimp, swordfish, Crab, Lobster Oysters, Clams,scallops and squid and Mussels. Seafood in the market fresh.
Here you can order seafood and it can be shipped or delivered.

Farmers Market
Pike Place Market in Seattle started in 1907. Here farmers and customers meet directly.

Crafts Market
Crafts market is open from 10 AM to 4 PM from Sunday to Wednesday. From Thursday, Friday, Saturday Crafts market is open from 10 AM till 5 PM.

Restaurant
While you are in Seattle downtow, have lunch or dinner in Pike Place market. You can seat and eat in restaurant that provide water views.
You can enjoy authentic northwest seafood. There are French, Italian, Japanese, Chinese restaurant in pike place.

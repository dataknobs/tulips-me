What is Ticket Price for Tulip Festival - Skagit Valley

Admission Ticket is $5 per person and $4 for those with military ID. 

Children under age of 10  are FREE.  Parking is FREE

 

The display garden and gift shop are open year round! Admission for the garden is only charged during the spring bloom period.  Rest of year entry to garden and gift shop is free.

Describe Tulips festival across world? There are many tulip festivals held around the world, the most notable being the Tulip Festival in Holland. This festival is held annually in the Netherlands and features a parade of floats decorated with tulips, as well as a variety of other activities and entertainment. There are many special places to see tulips across the world. Here are a few of the most popular: Keukenhof, Netherlands is the world's largest flower garden, and it's home to over 7 million tulips. The gardens are open from late March to mid-May, and they're a must-see for any tulip lover. The International Istanbul Tulip Festival takes place in Turkey each April, and it features over 10 million tulips in bloom. The festival is a great way to experience Turkish culture and see some of the most beautiful tulips in the world. The Indira Gandhi Memorial Tulip Garden in Kashmir, India, is home to over 2 million tulips. The gardens are open from March to May, and they offer stunning views of the Himalayan mountains. The Skagit Valley Tulip Festival in Washington State, USA, is the largest tulip festival in North America. The festival takes place each April, and it features over 4 million tulips in bloom. The Tonami Tulip Fair in Tonami, Japan, is a smaller festival, but it's no less beautiful. The festival takes place each April, and it features over 1 million tulips in bloom.
